Manual for JUMP room [x y]

jump can be used for jumping directly into a loaded room. External room jumps are not supported yet.

Parameters:
room: The internal room name, with underscore instead of spaces.
x (optional): The initial x position when the room can scroll. Default is 0.
y (optional): The initial y position when the room can scroll. Default is 0.
